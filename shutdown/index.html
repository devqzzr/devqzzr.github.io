Qzzr is shutting down on Oct. 31, 2022

We’re sorry to announce Qzzr will be shutting down at the end of the day of October 31, 2022.

Here's what you need to know:

  1. After October 31, you'll no longer be able to access the Qzzr app to edit quizzes or export leads. Any embedded quizzes will no longer work.
  2. You'll need to export any quiz or lead data before October 31. Instructions for exporting are available here.
  3. You’ll be refunded for any service you paid for after October 31. If you are on a monthly plan, you won’t be billed for October. If you are on a yearly plan, we’ll prorate and refund your remaining balance.

If you’re looking for a Qzzr replacement, we recommend Typeform.

Thank you for subscribing to Qzzr. We appreciate the support you’ve given us over the years and we wish you all the best. If you have any questions or need any help, please contact us at support@qzzr.com.
